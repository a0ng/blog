March 7, 2025

I've been thinking about the intersection of digital art and traditional media. There's something magical about bringing these worlds together - the precision of digital tools with the unpredictability of analog methods.

Yesterday I experimented with printing digital designs onto handmade paper. The results were unexpected and delightful. The imperfections in the paper created interesting textures that couldn't be replicated digitally.
